Non-Fiction

Nonfiction is literature that, regardless of the subject matter, has a simple goal: to provide information. It should be based on facts and conclusions of the author’s research or expertise, as opposed to the creativity of the author’s imagination.

Granted, storytelling skills are required when writing a great work of nonfiction, but the content is still expected to be accurate. In addition to this, modern nonfiction is prose — writing that sounds like everyday speech and is not written in meter, like poetry.
